About the Founder

Cybervault Solutions

Sunil Yadav
Founder & CEO, Cybervault Solutions

Cybervault Solutions was founded by Sunil Yadav, a cybersecurity professional with strong hands-on expertise in enterprise Data Loss Prevention (DLP) technologies.

Sunil brings practical experience in DLP consulting, implementation, policy configuration, false-positive tuning, server management, version upgrades, performance optimization, and compliance readiness across large enterprise environments.

Having worked closely with business and security teams, the focus is on delivering security solutions that protect sensitive data without disrupting productivity.

Mission: To help organizations prevent data leakage through practical, scalable, and business-aligned DLP solutions.
